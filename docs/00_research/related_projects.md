# Research - Related Projects

## Related work 

A key reference for our methodology is the work by Dedeepya et al. (2024) titled Sentiment Analysis for Online Shopping Reviews Using Machine Learning.[4]

Summary: This study addresses the nuances of e-commerce feedback, such as sarcasm and context shifts, which can confuse basic classifiers. The authors primarily explored advanced deep learning architectures, including BERT, BART, and GPT, to maximize accuracy.

The Gap: While Dedeepya et al. demonstrate the power of Transformers, these models require substantial computational resources (GPUs) and training time. Our project seeks to address the computational efficiency gap. We propose that well-tuned traditional algorithms can achieve satisfactory performance for binary classification without the massive overhead of deep learning.
